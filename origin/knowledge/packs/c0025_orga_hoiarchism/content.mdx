## Level 2: Universal Invariants (U1–U8)

These are not principles. They are the minimum organs a reality-bound system must possess
to avoid delusion, rot, and blobness. Each is expressed in atomic form, stability role,
and failure signature.

### 2.1 Invariant Table

| ID | Name | Atomic | Role | Failure Signature |
|----|------|--------|------|-------------------|
| **U1** | Existence | Something is | Anchors all action in a real arena | Pure narrative drift; decisions detached from any check |
| **U2** | Constraint | Limits exist; boundaries are real | Prevents infinite-sprawl incoherence | "Anything goes," then collapse |
| **U3** | Difference | Not all states are the same | Enables discrimination, learning, and choice | Moral and operational mush; no meaningful correction |
| **U4** | Identity | A thing is itself and not everything else | Makes responsibility and ownership traceable | Blobness; role fusion; "nobody is accountable" |

| **U6** | Time | Change happens; order matters | Enables compounding, causality, and horizon truth | Short-term theatre; delayed harm treated as "unexpected" |
| **U7** | Consequence | Actions land; the world answers | Forces ethics and engineering to face outcomes | "Good intentions" used as immunity from harm |
| **U8** | Correction (Feedback) | Errors must be detectable and repairable | Keeps the system from becoming self-sealing | Punished truth; crisis-by-crisis living; reality-debt |

### 2.2 Orga Claim

> Any system missing any one of these invariants may still operate, but it cannot remain

### 4.2 Universal Stan-dens (UD0–UD5)

A **Stan-den** is the density of Stans required per unit progress: how "thick" the

| ID | Name | Description | Examples |

## Level 5: Hoiarchism and Its Holism

**Hoiarchism** is the governing discipline that keeps Orga true across scales.

```
Orga says:        "build an organismal system with organs"
Hoiarchism says:  "ensure those organs preserve truth, consequence, and sacred
                   dignity—no matter how large the organism grows"
```

### 5.1 The Seven Mandates of Hoiarchic Holism

Hoiarchism's Holism is the seeing faculty that prevents self-deception:

| Mandate | Positive | Negative (failure) |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| Parts/Wholes | See parts without losing wholes | Reductionism that loses the system |
| Persons | See wholes without sacrificing persons | Holism that erases individuals |
| Relations | Trace relations instead of blaming symptoms | Symptom-fixing without causal sight |
| Feedback | Protect feedback instead of punishing it | Truth-suppression; reality-debt |
| Costs | Name costs instead of exporting them | Externalization; hidden harm |
| Time | Hold time horizons instead of worshiping immediacy | Short-termism; compounding ignored |
| Leverage | Hunt leverage instead of performing theatre | Performative action; wasted energy |

### 5.2 The Key Distinction

**Holism**: "Everything is connected."
**Hoiarchism**: "Everything is connected *and* everything is accountable."

Holism without governance is aesthetics. Hoiarchism is Holism with teeth.
